# Privacy Policy

*Last Updated: February 10, 2026*

This Privacy Policy has been prepared to explain how your personal data is collected, processed, and protected by the Kelam mobile application.

## 1. Data Collected and Purposes of Processing
The following data may be processed within the scope of the application:
- **Account Information**: Email and user ID obtained during registration (for authentication).
- **Location Data**: Precise geographic location (GPS) from your device for automatic calculation of prayer times and detection of Qibla direction (only with your permission and while the app is in use).
- **Usage Data**: Information about feature usage, premium status, and in-app interactions.
- **AI Conversations**: Chat history and feedback from the AI Assistant (to improve service quality).
- **Technical Information**: Device model, OS version, and unique device identifiers (IDFA/AAID).
- **Analytics and Crash Reports**: Data used to measure app performance and resolve technical issues.

## 2. Third-Party Services and Partners
To provide our services efficiently, we collaborate with the following providers:
- **Google AdMob**: Uses device identifiers (IDFA, AAID) to deliver advertisements. In the free version, it may show you personalized ads.
- **Adapty**: Used for managing subscription transactions, payment verification, and access to premium features.
- **Firebase (Google)**: Used for analytics, push notifications (Cloud Messaging), crash reporting (Crashlytics), and data storage (Firestore/Auth).
- **AI Providers**: Text inputs may be processed anonymously to power chat features.

## 3. Advertising and Tracking Permissions
In compliance with Apple’s App Tracking Transparency (ATT) and Google’s advertising policies:
- Users can change their ad personalization preferences at any time via **Profile > Legal Information > Manage Ad Privacy**.
- Advertising identifiers are used only with explicit user consent.

## 4. Notifications
The app may send push notifications for prayer times and important updates. You can manage these preferences in your device settings or via the notification menu within the app.

## 5. Data Security and Retention
Your data is protected using industry-standard encryption methods to prevent unauthorized access. Personal data such as your dhikrs and notes are stored only on your device. Server-side data (account info, AI history) is maintained in high-security cloud infrastructures (Firebase).

## 6. Children's Privacy
Our services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers.

## 7. Account Deletion
Users can delete their accounts at any time through the in-app settings. Upon deletion, all personal data associated with your account on our servers (email, AI history, etc.) will be permanently anonymized or removed.

## 8. User Rights (GDPR/KVKK)
Users have the right to access, correct, delete, or object to the processing of their data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email provided below.
